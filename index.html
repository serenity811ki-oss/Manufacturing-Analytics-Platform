Platform capabilities

Everything the plant floor needs, in one SQL-backed system.

Built on a normalized relational schema, so every KPI you see traces back to an auditable query — not a spreadsheet guess.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live machine status, cycle counts, and shift activity streamed to the dashboard as it happens on the line — no manual refresh required.

Database Integration

A normalized SQL schema with indexed foreign keys across machines, production, downtime, and maintenance — built for fast, reliable joins at scale.

KPI Dashboard

Availability, performance, and quality rolled into OEE, with plant-wide and per-machine views side by side.

Production Analytics

Trend, throughput, and shift-level analysis across lines and machines, filterable by date range, line, or status.

Predictive Maintenance

Failure-code tracking, MTBF, and MTTR that flag machines heading toward a breakdown before it costs a shift.

Report Generation

Export CSV, Excel, or a print-ready PDF summary on demand — for shift handoffs, audits, or leadership reviews.

How it works

From raw machine data to a decision, in four steps.

The pipeline is deliberately simple: connect your data, and the platform handles ingestion, calculation, and presentation.

01

Connect

Point the platform at your production SQL database, or start from the included normalized schema.

02

Ingest

Production runs, downtime events, maintenance work orders, and quality inspections flow in continuously.

03

Analyze

SQL-driven analytics compute OEE, MTBF/MTTR, and downtime root-cause Pareto as new data arrives.

04

Visualize & Act

Dashboards and reports surface the numbers your team needs, filtered by line, shift, or machine — ready to export or share.
